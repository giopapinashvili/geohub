Camera Proof System

The most reliable way to prove you actually visited a place. GPS coordinates, timestamps and AI analysis combined into a single trust signal.

How It Works
1
Open GeoHub at the location
The app captures your GPS coordinates and compares them to the business address within a 50m radius.
2
Take a photo in-app
Use the built-in camera — screenshots and imported photos are rejected. The shutter embeds GPS and a trusted timestamp.
3
AI image analysis
Our model checks that the scene matches the venue category and that no duplicate or stock image was used.
4
Before & After comparison
Returning visitors can submit comparison shots over time — improving both your score and the business listing quality.
5
Anti-fake detection
Metadata is stripped and re-signed by GeoHub servers. Edited EXIF data, VPN GPS spoofing, and emulator environments are flagged.
Who Benefits

For Businesses

  • Reviews rank higher in search if backed by Camera Proof
  • Fake competitor reviews are easier to flag and remove
  • Real visitor photos auto-populate your listing gallery
  • Trust score feeds into GeoHub algorithm ranking

For Creators

  • Camera Proof badge appears on all your reviews
  • Unlocks Camera Proof verification level (300+ pts)
  • Brands prefer creators with verified visit history
  • Each proof photo earns +8 XP to your explorer score
