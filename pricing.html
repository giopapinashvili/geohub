Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about GeoHub plans
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing period. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Is the Free plan truly free forever?
Absolutely. The Free plan is always available with no time limits. We believe everyone should be able to explore GeoHub's city network. Paid plans unlock advanced features when you need them.
What payment methods do you accept?
GeoHub accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), as well as TBC Pay, Bank of Georgia Pay, and popular Georgian mobile wallets. Apple Pay and Google Pay coming soon.
How does the yearly discount work?
When you choose yearly billing, you pay upfront for 12 months at a discounted rate — typically saving 17–25% compared to monthly billing. The full year is charged on your start date and renews annually.
Can businesses have multiple locations on one plan?
The Growth and Premium Business plans cover a single location. For chains or multi-location businesses, contact our partnerships team for a custom enterprise plan with bulk pricing and centralized analytics.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your profile, reviews, check-ins, and earned trust score are never deleted. If you cancel a paid plan, you'll revert to the Free tier and lose access to paid features — but all your content remains intact.
Do Creator plans share revenue with GeoHub?
Creator Pro and Studio plans give you full access to the brand deals marketplace. GeoHub takes a 12% platform fee on completed brand deals. Direct tip/donation features have a 5% fee. Subscription revenue from fans is 0% fee for Studio members.
Is there a student or NGO discount?
Yes! Students with a valid Georgian university email get 50% off any personal plan. Registered NGOs and civic organizations get Creator Pro free of charge as part of GeoHub's Patriot civic initiative. Contact support to apply.
